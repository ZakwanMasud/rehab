About Us

All-In-One Employment Services

All-In-One Employment Services is a non-profit that assists young people in finding their first employment. Between applying for a job and securing a job,there are various stages that everyone must take. Writing a CV, which must be completed before looking for a job, applying for a Tax File Number (TFN) and an own super savings account, searching for, and applying for employment,dressing for the interview, and doing the interview are all steps that must be completed. And we will help young people to subsequently manage their own banking and industrial relations affairs. To find a job, a young person must deal with dozens of organizations to complete these processes, which might be perplexing at times. All-In-One Employment Services has gathered all these resources in one spot to make it easier for young people. Our goal is to make the entire hiring process as simple as possible for you. We will assist you with understanding taxation and obtaining a Tax File Number, as well as tell you how much wage you are entitled to and everything else you need to know as an employee.

Applying for a Tax File Number (TFN)

Click the “Apply Now” button and fill out the form and press submit. By doing this your application for TFN will be submitted to the Australian Tax Office and you will get a mail with a receipt of submission and with an application summary. Then you should start searching for jobs and applying for jobs using the job search button.

Super Fund

SuperFund is a way of saving for retirement. Your employer must pay a percentage of your earnings into your super account, and your super fund invests the money until you retire. There are lots of different super funds out there, and different types of accounts.
